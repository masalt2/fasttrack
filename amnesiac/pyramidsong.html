Pyramid Song

Pyramid Song is the second track on Amnesiac, Radiohead's fifth studio album. Although it doesn't sound like it, it's written entirely in 4/4. It makes use of a dotted quarter note rhythm. The lenth of each chord in eighth notes for every bar would be 3, 3, 4, 3, 3.


The lyrics go:

black eyed angels swam with me
a moon full of stars and astral cars
and all things i used to see

all my lovers were there with me
all my past and future
and we all went to heaven in a little row boat
there was nothing to fear, nothing to doubt
